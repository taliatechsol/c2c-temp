# c2c Candidate Target Personas

The campus2corporate (c2c) agency focuses on high-impact technical talent. 
We are looking for roles that match these candidate profiles:

## 1. The AI Research Engineer
- **Skills:** PyTorch, Transformers, CUDA, Python.
- **Goal:** Designing and training large-scale models.
- **Preference:** Foundational model work over wrapper apps.

## 2. The Systems Architect
- **Skills:** Distributed systems, Kubernetes, Go/Rust, Cloud Infrastructure.
- **Goal:** Building the infrastructure to serve models at scale.

## 3. The Full-Stack AI Builder
- **Skills:** React, FastAPI, LangGraph, RAG.
- **Goal:** Building intelligent agents and enterprise-grade AI applications.
